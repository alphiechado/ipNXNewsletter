Update on Appraisals

Dear All,

Trust this mail meets you well.
Apologies for the late update on Q1 2022 appraisals.
Appraisals are expected to be carried out every quarter (for employees on Assistant Manager grade level and above) and Half-yearly (for Officers and Senior Officers). However, at this time, we deem it best to put Q1 2022 on hold. This is mainly to tighten the divisional and departmental SLAs. We have the approval of Management to combine the Q1 and Q2 appraisal for this year. Hence, for 2022 appraisal cycle, we will have the following 3 appraisal periods;
1. H1 (Q1&Q2) 2022 Appraisal (for all confirmed staff, all grade levels) in July
2. Q3 2022 Appraisal (for Assistant Managers & above only) in October
3. Q4/H2 Appraisals (for all confirmed staff) in January
Please remember that participation in appraisals is compulsory...
